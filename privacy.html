Privacy Policy for DeskSmash

Effective date: April 24, 2026

DeskSmash is a macOS desktop overlay app developed by Yigit Arslan.

Overview

DeskSmash is designed as a playful, fictional desktop experience and does not simulate real-world actions or collect behavioral data.

The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or payment details.

Data Collection

DeskSmash does not:

  • collect personal data
  • track user activity
  • include analytics or advertising SDKs
  • capture, record, store, or transmit screen content
  • access or record camera or microphone input

System Interaction

DeskSmash uses standard macOS system features (such as mouse input and limited window-level information) solely to provide its interactive overlay functionality.

These features are used only to make the app work as intended and are not used to monitor or collect the contents of your screen or personal data.

Communication

If you contact the developer by email, your email address and any information you provide will be used only to respond to your inquiry.

Data Sharing

DeskSmash does not sell, share, or disclose personal data to third parties for advertising or tracking purposes.

Data Storage

DeskSmash does not operate user accounts or backend services and does not maintain a database of personal user information.

Third-Party Processing

Device-level diagnostics or crash reports, if enabled, may be processed by Apple Inc. under their own terms and privacy practices.

Your Control

You can stop all data processing related to DeskSmash at any time by closing or uninstalling the app.
