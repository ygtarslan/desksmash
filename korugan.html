(iOS Interface)

AES-256-GCM

File content is locally encrypted using Apple CryptoKit with authenticated encryption and a random nonce.

Local Keychain

Your 256-bit vault master key never leaves your device. It is non-synchronizable and locked to the device.

Smart Decryption

Text and images decrypt directly into memory. Temporary cleartext files for QuickLook are instantly wiped upon locking.

Share Inbox

The Share Extension writes incoming items into an App Group inbox before the main app firmly encrypts them.

Built for extreme local privacy.

Korugan has no cloud sync, no tracking frameworks, and no communication with our servers.

We designed Korugan entirely from the ground up for individuals who prioritize physical, local-device security. Storage operates heavily protected and encrypted natively inside the iOS sandbox.

Cryptography,
at the foundation.

Every single file that enters your vault is converted into an encrypted blob formatted strictly as nonce + ciphertext + authentication tag. This protects both the absolute confidentiality and exact integrity of your file data at rest.

No backend involved. Period.

SymmetricKey

A single master key generated directly on your iPhone.

Zero CloudKit

The vault operates completely airgapped from iCloud storage mechanics.

Sidecar Blobs

Even automatically generated thumbnail previews are securely encrypted using the identical pipeline.

Metadata Boundary

Filename, size, and type info remain unencrypted in SwiftData purely for extremely fast local indexing and search.
